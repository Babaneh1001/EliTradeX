Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Elitradex?

Elitradex is a comprehensive facilitate seamless trade and procurement for businesses and consumers. We offer a wide range of products from global manufacturers and provide solutions to manage foreign currency invoices and payments efficiently.

How does Elitradex's Trade and Invoice MGR work?

Trade and Invoice MGR allows businesses to manage their foreign currency (FCY) invoices and payments. You can purchase goods in foreign currencies and settle your invoices in local currency (NGN), helping you manage FX obligations without the hassle.

What is the FindR platform?

FindR is our sourcing tool that connects users with a vast pool of manufacturers and suppliers globally. It provides market insights and a variety of options to help users make informed sourcing decisions.

What are the benefits of Elitradex for my business?

Elitradex offers a single platform to manage all trading activities, order placements, and invoice settlements. You can trade with foreign suppliers in FCY while settling payments in NGN, ensuring smooth and efficient business operations.

How can I create an account on Elitradex?

To create an account, visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Fill in the required details, verify your email, and you'll be ready to start using our services.

How do I find products on Elitradex?

Use the FindR platform to search for products. You can filter results based on categories, manufacturers, or specific product attributes to find exactly what you need from our extensive database.

What payment methods are supported?

We support various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets. Our Trade and Invoice MGR also allows for payments in NGN for invoices issued in FCY.

Is there customer support available if I need help?

Yes, we offer robust customer support. You can reach our support team via email, phone, or live chat on our website. We're here to help you with any issues or queries you might have.

How secure is my data with Elitradex?

We take data security very seriously and employ advanced encryption methods to protect your personal and financial information. Our platform is designed to ensure the highest levels of security and privacy for our users.
